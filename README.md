# Introduction
The D-robotics RDK X5 Magicbox is an all-in-one multimodal AI platform that integrates vision, hearing, and motion into a compact, ready-to-use system. Powered by the high-performance RDK X5 main controller (up to 10 TOPS), it combines a stereo depth camera, programmable RGB status lights, microphone, speaker and dual servo-driven rocker arms, enabling natural “see, hear, speak, and move” interactions. Designed for teaching, research, and creative development, the Magicbox offers an out-of-the-box experience that accelerates the development of intelligent multimodal AI applications.
# Features
* High‑Performance Computing Core

Equipped with the RDK X5 8GB Developer Kit, the Magicbox delivers up to 10 TOPS of AI computing power, enabling seamless execution of complex neural networks and advanced algorithms such as Transformer and RWKV for demanding edge AI tasks.

* Integrated Stereo Depth Vision

The built-in GS130WI stereo depth camera provides synchronized binocular vision with adjustable tilt. Pre-integrated depth vision algorithms and rich visual processing cases make it ideal for applications like 3D perception, obstacle detection, and spatial awareness.

* Expressive Physical & Visual Interaction

Features dual programmable servo rocker arms for smooth, gesture‑based physical interaction, along with four WS2812B RGB status lights supporting 24‑bit full‑color customization—perfect for creating dynamic feedback and immersive user experiences.

* Intelligent Voice & Seamless Development Experience

Integrated high-sensitivity microphone and speaker enable voice-controlled actuation and deep integration with large language models for building intelligent AI voice assistants. With out-of-the-box examples, RDK Studio support, and one-click OpenClaw installation, developers can quickly prototype AI-powered hardware interactions.
